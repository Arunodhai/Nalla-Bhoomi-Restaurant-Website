About Nalla Bhoomi

At Nalla Bhoomi, every meal is rooted in honest Kerala cooking. Located near KC Center at High School Junction, Karunagappally, we serve travelers and local families with signature regional dishes, comfortable seating, and hospitality that feels personal.

From authentic traditional preparations to practical conveniences like parking, EV charging, and clean amenities, the goal is simple: make every visit easy, memorable, and worth returning for.
