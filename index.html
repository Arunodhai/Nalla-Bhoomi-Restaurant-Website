Our Story

A Good Earth,
A Great Table.

Born from a deep-rooted respect for the soil, Nalla Bhoomi Family Restaurant is more than just a place to eat in Karunagappally—it is a movement shaped by the belief that the "Good Earth" provides everything when treated with honor. Our journey was inspired by a grand inauguration by the legendary Chef Pillai, whose commitment to Kerala’s culinary legacy led us to revive the forgotten, authentic flavors of our land.

From the aroma of our signature Kizhi Biryani steamed in banana leaves to soulful classics like Beef Masala, Duck Mappas, and Kuttanad Chicken Curry with flaky Porottas, every plate is crafted to feel like a memory from home. Whether you are stopping for a homely breakfast of Appam and Idiyappam or a traditional Fish Biryani, our nadan specials offer a true taste of South Indian heritage.

Situated as a premier pitstop on the NH66, we bridge tradition with the future. With our pioneering "No Food Waste" mission and high-speed EV charging facilities, we invite families and travelers to recharge both themselves and their journeys with conscience and care. At Nalla Bhoomi, every detail is designed with purpose, ensuring that all that lingers after your visit is the memory of a perfect meal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Nalla Bhoomi is widely regarded as a landmark destination for traditional South Indian cuisine on the Karunagappally NH66 stretch. Our menu features regional specialties and slow-cooked delicacies that capture the true heritage of Kerala, making us the preferred choice for both local families and highway travelers.
Yes, we are conveniently located near High School Junction Karunagappally on the NH66 highway. We offer spacious parking, clean restrooms, and a dedicated prayer room, providing a premium and comfortable pitstop for travelers passing through the Kollam district.
To support sustainable travel, Nalla Bhoomi features a high-speed GOEC EV charging station on-site. Guests can enjoy a traditional meal while their electric vehicles are quickly charged, making us a key eco-friendly hub for long-distance drivers in Kerala.
We pride ourselves on being a premier family restaurant with a welcoming atmosphere for everyone from toddlers to grandparents. With a diverse menu that caters to all palates and a strict commitment to hygiene, we ensure a comfortable and memorable dining experience for every generation.
In line with our name, which means "Good Earth," we are dedicated to eco-conscious practices. We run an active "No Food Waste" campaign and encourage mindful dining, ensuring that our operations respect the environment and the local community we serve.
Absolutely. We are famous for our signature Kizhi Biryani (steamed in banana leaves) and traditional favorites like Duck Roast, Chemmeen (Prawns) Roast, and Karimeen Pollichathu. Our kitchen specializes in the coastal flavors of Kuttanad and Malabar, using hand-ground spices for an authentic taste.
Yes, you can enjoy the taste of Nalla Bhoomi from the comfort of your home. We are available for online ordering and home delivery via Swiggy and Zomato in the Karunagappally area. Simply search for "Nalla Bhoomi" on the apps to browse our full menu and current offers.
